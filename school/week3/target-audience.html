Target Audience Description

The target audience for this website is anyone that could look be in or around these cities. This can include those that are looking at visiting or even those that currently live in them. Weather also applies to any age group so anyone from young teens to older adults would find my site informative but also easy to use. Also, this could apply to those that are in and around these cities as well so they can know if the storm is headed which way and they will be able to see what is happening.
