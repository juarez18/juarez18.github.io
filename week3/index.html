Site Name: Nice Weather or Not

This site's purpose will be to provide weather and other pertinent information on 3 select Idaho cities. This information will be to let visitors to our website provide accurate and up to date weather information on these cities while also providing an image of what the current conditions will look like so they can make more informed decisions on what activities they can accomplish today.
